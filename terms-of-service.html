Terms of Service

Clear expectations create confident partnerships.

These terms explain how Fidelis Agency collaborates with clients, protects intellectual property, and manages payments. Every statement applies to all projects unless superseded by a signed Master Service Agreement.

Effective date: January 17, 2025 Last reviewed: January 17, 2025

1. Engagement & acceptance

By approving a proposal, estimate, or invoice you agree to these terms.

  • The scope, timeline, and deliverables are outlined in the latest signed proposal.
  • Changes requested after approval require a written change order that may alter fees or deadlines.
  • Either party may end an engagement with 14 days' written notice if obligations are not met.

2. Responsibilities

Successful projects rely on timely collaboration.

  • Fidelis Agency provides expert strategy, creative, and technical execution.
  • Clients provide assets, reviews, and approvals within the agreed timeline.
  • Delays caused by missing feedback may shift launch dates and incur standby fees.

3. Fees & payment

All fees are quoted in AUD unless stated otherwise.

  • Projects typically require a 50% deposit with the remainder due prior to launch or delivery.
  • Retainers and marketing engagements are invoiced monthly in advance.
  • Late payments (7+ days) may pause work and accrue a 2% monthly service charge.

4. Intellectual property

Ownership is simple and transparent:

  • Clients own final approved deliverables once all invoices are paid.
  • Fidelis Agency retains rights to pre-existing tools, frameworks, or methodologies.
  • We reserve the right to showcase finished work in portfolios and case studies unless confidentiality is requested.

5. Confidentiality & data

Both parties agree to protect sensitive information shared throughout the engagement.

  • Documents are only shared with team members directly involved in the project.
  • Any third-party subcontractor is bound by similar confidentiality and data protection terms.
  • See our Privacy Policy for details on data handling.

6. Warranties, limitation & force majeure

We stand behind our work, but some limits are necessary.

  • We warrant that services will be delivered with professional skill and care.
  • Liability for any claim is capped at the total fees paid for the relevant project.
  • Neither party is liable for delays caused by events outside reasonable control (force majeure).
